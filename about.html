About Plaintext

Who We Are

Plaintext is a team of passionate professionals dedicated to creating software that simplifies and enhances the way people and organizations manage their finances, cybersecurity, productivity, and customer service. We believe that by making best practices second nature, we can help build a safer, more efficient, and more trustworthy digital world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower people and organizations everywhere with the knowledge, tools, and habits needed to thrive in finance, cybersecurity, productivity, and customer service.

What We Do

Plaintext develops software products that make financial management, digital security, productivity, and customer service simpler, safer, and more effective for everyone. We believe in broad, accessible solutions that help individuals, teams, and organizations build strong habits and resilient strategies across these four essential areas.

  • Financial: Tools and guidance for secure, confident financial management and fraud prevention.
  • Cybersecurity: Solutions that help you understand, identify, and defend against digital threats, making security second nature.
  • Productivity: Software that streamlines workflows, fosters good habits, and helps you achieve more with less friction.
  • Customer Service: Empowering organizations to deliver responsive, trustworthy, and effective support to their customers.

Our Principles & Resources

  • Guidance for building strategies in finance, security, productivity, and service
  • Simulated scenarios and training for learning and awareness
  • Visibility and tracking tools for assets, tasks, and customer needs
  • Support for digital safety, fraud prevention, and operational excellence
  • Best practices for verifying identities, protecting data, and building trust

Why It Matters
